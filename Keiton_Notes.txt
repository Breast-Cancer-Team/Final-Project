Recommended Screening Guidelines:
 Mammography: X-ray of the breast
 Age of women: 40 - 45 and above
 Women at high risk: /or have breast cancer family history

Risk Factors:
 Age: 80% of BC are found over age of 50
 Personal history of BC
 Family history of BC
 Genetic factor(BRCA1 AND BRCA2)
 Childbearing and menstrual history
